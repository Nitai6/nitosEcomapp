## Qualification thresholds

- **Active ads minimum:** 6–7 (10+ better)
- **Page transparency age:** older = more credible (avoid brand-new pages)
- **Keyword breadth:** at least 5 different keywords/variations per platform

## Search platforms

1. Meta Ads Library: `https://www.facebook.com/ads/library`
2. TikTok Creative Center / search
3. Google search + direct site discovery

## Revenue assumption formula

```
Revenue ≈ Ad Spend × (AOV × CVR ÷ CPC)
```

Inputs:
- **Ad Spend:** estimated from active ad count × avg CPM × estimated impressions
- **AOV:** average order value (read product page + bundle pricing)
- **CVR:** conversion rate (industry benchmark or visible cart count)
- **CPC:** cost per click (industry benchmark for vertical)
