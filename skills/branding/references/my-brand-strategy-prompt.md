# Owner's Brand Strategy Prompt (Prompt 1)

The full prompt is in `../SKILL.md` under "Owner's playbook (verbatim) → Prompt 1 — Brand Strategy Blueprint" to keep a single source of truth.

## Purpose

Generates the **Brand Strategy Blueprint** — 8 deliverables that anchor everything else.

## Deliverables (output structure)

1. **Brand Positioning** — problem we solve, why we feel safer, what makes us different from AliExpress clones
2. **Core Brand Idea** — one concept, the "enemy", the transformation
3. **Brand Personality** — 3–4 traits, how we talk vs how we never talk
4. **Messaging Framework** — main VP, 3 key benefits (emotional + functional), objection killers
5. **Tone of Voice** — writing rules, forbidden words, examples (homepage / product page / ad)
6. **Dropshipping Brand Story** — origin, why customers trust us
7. **Visual Direction** — store look/feel, colors, typography, image style
8. **Brand Trust System** — trust signals, what makes it feel legit, what kills trust

## Output

JSON to `state/branding/brand-strategy-blueprint.json`. One key per deliverable.
