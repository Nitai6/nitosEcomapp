# Deliverability — Shrink-to-Win Protocol

## Metrics goals (always)
- Open Rate: **>45%**
- Click Rate: **>1%**
- Bounce Rate: **<1%**
- Spam Complaint Rate: **<0.01%**
- Unsubscribe Rate: **<0.5%**

## Footer trick
Add invisible HTML text in footer to maintain text-to-image ratio (image-heavy emails deliverable without cluttering design).

## Shrink-to-Win — 6-week recovery (when Open <45%)

| Phase | Duration | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Weeks 1–3 | Pause all non-essential auto. Campaigns ONLY to 14-day-engaged. Boost opens artificially. |
| 2 | Hit target | Wait until 14-day group consistently 50%+ open |
| 3 | Weeks 3–6+ | Gradually add 30-day → 60-day → 90-day engaged back into regular sends |
| 4 | Maintain | Only move to next tier once current maintains 40–50% open. **Never** send regular campaigns to >90-day-unengaged |

> In short: stop emailing unengaged people until open rates excellent, then slowly widen the net.
