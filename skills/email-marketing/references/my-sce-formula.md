# S.C.E Formula — Every Email Must Follow

Owner's hard rule: every email — flow or campaign — must pass S.C.E.

## S — Skimmable
The customer isn't reading the morning newspaper. Couple seconds of attention. They see the point — relatable → stay; not relatable → leave.

> Avoid blocks of text. **Bold main points.** Keep it short and to the point.

## C — Clear & Concise
The customer's IQ drops ~50% the second they open socials. Zombie brain mode, dopamine-craving. Not clear → won't understand. Not concise → won't bother.

> Short and sweet. **One takeaway per email** (sub-points may support, but ONE main takeaway).

## E — Engaging
Customer craves dopamine — give it to them. Engaging emails trigger dopamine → positive brand association → future orders.

> Punchy (short but full of power). Informational. Entertaining.

## Avoid

- Unnecessary info
- Too-long copy
- Too many points in one email
- Don't tell subscribers they'll get "exclusive access, insights, sneak peeks" — they already know
