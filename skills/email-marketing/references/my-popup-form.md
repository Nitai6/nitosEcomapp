# Pop-up Form (one-time setup)

## Mistakes to avoid
- Offer isn't appealing
- Too many requests from user
- No (or wrong) time delay
- Slow load (compress images)
- Form too small/tiny
- Too much copy
- Too much going on
- Offer unclear
- Too easy to close

## What to do RIGHT

### Establish offer
- **Curiosity angle** — "find my X"
- **Free guide** — "get X guide for free"
- **Giveaway** — enter for X

### Form types (use one of these only)
- **Micro-commit quiz**
- **The Classic**
- **Uncover your X**

### Winning checklist
- [ ] Only one input per step
- [ ] Time delay 4–12s
- [ ] Loads instantly after delay (no lag)
- [ ] Covers ≥75% of screen
- [ ] <10 words of copy
- [ ] Offer is the largest element on form
- [ ] No "X" button — instead "Close Form" button **under** submit

### A/B testing (one variable at a time)
- Offer
- Form type
- Close-form function
- Time delay
- Photo content (colors etc.)
- Text copy
