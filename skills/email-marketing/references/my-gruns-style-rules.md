# Gruns Design Style — Reference Rules

## Why Gruns
Gruns is the visual benchmark — clean, modern ecommerce email design. We **copy them** (per owner).

## Core rules

- **Ease of click** — CTAs obvious, easy to tap
- **Skimability** — visual hierarchy, scannable layout
- **Branding** — consistent brand look (colors, fonts) across every email
- **Mobile-first** — vast majority of opens on mobile

## Infographics to use

1. Checklists
2. Icon graphics
3. Features diagrams
4. Timelines
5. Numbered lists
6. Comparison charts
7. Tables
8. Flow charts
9. Graphs

## Color & typography
- Pull from `state/branding/brand-strategy-blueprint.json` → Visual Direction
- Never deviate from approved palette/fonts
- Forbidden words check from brand strategy applies to subject lines + preheaders too
