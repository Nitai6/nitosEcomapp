Drop headshot photos of yourself here (PNG or JPG).

These are sent to Gemini as reference images when generating Instagram reel covers. The AI uses your likeness to composite you into the cover.

If this folder is empty, the skill will fall back to headshots in the youtube-thumbnail skill directory.

Tips for best results:
- Clear, well-lit face
- Multiple angles and expressions give more options
- Full-body or waist-up works well for vertical covers
- Transparent or simple backgrounds are ideal
