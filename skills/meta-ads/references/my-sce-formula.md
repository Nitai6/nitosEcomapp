# Owner's S.C.E Formula (verbatim — shared anchor)

Used by `ads-meta`, `ads-creative`, `emails-sms`, `ads-create`. Every ad and every email must follow this.

## S — Skimmable

The customer isn't sitting down and reading the ads in-depth like they would read the morning newspaper. We only have a couple seconds of our customer's attention. They usually see the point — if this is relatable for them they stay, not relatable — leave.

- Avoid blocks of text
- Bold main points
- Keep it short and to the point

## C — Clear & Concise

The customer's IQ drops by about 50% the second they open their socials. They're in zombie brain mode, they want dopamine. Therefore if we aren't clear with what we're giving them, they won't understand. If we aren't concise, they won't bother giving you the time.

- Short and sweet
- One takeaway per ad/email (can have multi sub/benefits/info supporting but **must have one main takeaway**)

## E — Engaging

The customer is craving dopamine — we need to give it to them. We want our ads to be engaging enough to trigger dopamine in the customer's brain. That will lead to a positive association with our brand and result in future orders and sales.

- Punchy (a punchy ad is short, but is full of power)
- Informational
- Entertaining

## Avoid

- Unnecessary info
- Too-long copy
- Too many points in one email/ad
- Don't tell subscribers they'll get "exclusive access, insights, sneak peeks" — they already know
