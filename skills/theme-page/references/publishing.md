# Publishing Operations — Theme-Page Manual

How to actually post: timing, captions, metadata, multi-platform workflow, and the technical mechanics that move content through the algorithm.

## Optimal posting times (2025-2026 data — adjust per audience timezone)

These are aggregate best-times. **Your audience's data overrides these** — pull insights monthly per platform and adjust.

### TikTok
- **Best**: Tue-Thu, 6-10am + 7-11pm local
- **Worst**: Sun before 9am, Sat 1-5pm
- **Cadence**: 3-5/day in growth, 1-3/day post-100k

### Instagram (Reels + posts)
- **Best**: Mon-Wed-Fri, 11am + 7pm local
- **Reels specifically**: 9pm-midnight tends to surge
- **Worst**: Saturday daytime
- **Cadence**: 1-2 Reels + 3-5 Stories daily

### YouTube
- **Long-form best**: Thu-Sat upload, 2-4pm local — algorithm caches over weekend prime
- **Shorts best**: same as TT — 6-10am + 7-11pm
- **Cadence**: 1-2 long-form/week, 1-3 Shorts/day

### Twitter / X
- **Best**: weekdays 8-10am + 6-9pm local
- **Worst**: weekends overall (lower volume but also lower competition)
- **Cadence**: 6-15 posts/day across single tweets, threads, replies

### Bluesky
- **Best**: similar to X but slightly later — 9-11am + 8-11pm
- **Cadence**: 3-8 posts/day

### Facebook
- **Best**: Tue-Thu, 1-4pm + 8-10pm local
- **Older audience peaks midday more than IG**
- **Cadence**: 1-3 page posts/day + group activity

### Pinterest
- **Best**: evenings 8-11pm + Saturdays
- **But**: pin lifecycle is months — exact-time matters less than freshness + SEO
- **Cadence**: 5-15 fresh pins/day in growth phase

## The unified publishing workflow

```
[Sunday — 60 min] PLANNING
  ├─ Pull last week's analytics across all platforms
  ├─ Identify top 3 + bottom 3 posts (per platform)
  ├─ Update hook bank with new winners
  └─ Plan next 7 days: 1 master idea per day → 7 platform variants

[Mon-Sun — 30-60 min/day] PRODUCTION
  ├─ Edit master content (CapCut)
  ├─ Export 4 aspect ratios (9:16, 1:1, 16:9, 1000×1500)
  ├─ Write 7 caption variants (one per platform)
  ├─ Pull 3-5 hashtags per platform from rotating bank
  └─ Schedule via Metricool/Buffer/Publer

[Daily — 15-30 min, post-publish] ENGAGEMENT
  ├─ Reply to first 60 min of comments (algorithm boost window)
  ├─ Cross-promote: Story share of new Reel, comment-reply videos on TT
  └─ Engage with 5-10 niche posts (comment, save, share)

[Weekly — 30 min] MAINTENANCE
  ├─ Check ban/strike status per account
  ├─ Update bio links if offer changed
  ├─ Refresh pinned content (top-3 most relevant evergreen)
  └─ Audit: any deleted posts? Comments needing response? DMs missed?
```

## Captions, hashtags, and metadata per platform

### TikTok
- **Caption**: 1-3 sentences. Restate hook + 1 CTA. 100-150 chars sweet spot.
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 in caption. Mix broad + specific.
- **Sound**: trending sound from TT library when appropriate (boosts reach 20-40%) — check `Discover` tab daily
- **Cover image**: select frame manually, NEVER let TT auto-pick. Use a clean frame with on-screen text.
- **Description tags**: tag relevant brands/creators if using their content (with permission)

### Instagram Reels
- **Caption**: 1-3 sentences. Save-bait or comment-trigger.
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 (Meta downranks 30-tag stuffing). Place in caption (not first comment) — both work, caption slightly better post-2024.
- **Audio**: use trending audio from Reels library when relevant, OR original audio (which you can credit-claim, others can use, drives discovery)
- **Cover**: custom 1080×1920 cover with bold text. Different from first frame. Consistent template = grid aesthetic.
- **Tag location** if niche-relevant (especially food/travel/local)
- **Collab feature**: collab Reels with other niche pages get 2-3× reach. Use it.

### YouTube Long-Form
- **Title**: 60-70 chars max. Front-load keyword. Power word in middle.
- **Description**: first 150 chars matter most (shown in feed preview). 
  - Para 1: 2-3 sentences, hook restated
  - Timestamps: 0:00 Intro, etc.
  - Resource links + affiliate
  - Generic SEO paragraph (200-300 words, niche keywords)
  - 3-5 hashtags last
- **Tags**: 5-15 keywords (less critical post-2020 but still indexed)
- **Thumbnail**: custom. 1280×720. Test 3 variants via YT native A/B.
- **End screen**: subscribe + next-video element (last 20s)
- **Cards**: 2-4 throughout video, link to related content
- **Chapters**: enable via timestamp formatting (drives watch time)

### YouTube Shorts
- **Title**: <40 chars. Hook only.
- **Description**: 1-2 sentences + #shorts + 2-3 niche tags
- **Cover**: not user-selectable for Shorts — first frame matters most
- **Vertical 9:16, under 60s**

### Twitter / X
- **Single tweet**: text only, 1 image/GIF max, period at end
- **Threads**: hook tweet pinned post-publish; numbered tweets; final CTA tweet
- **Image tweets**: vertical screenshot of formatted text bypasses character limit
- **No external links in tweet body** — kills reach. Drop link in reply.
- **Tag policy**: tag accounts when genuinely relevant; spam-tagging = shadowban
- **Premium**: paying for X Premium boosts reach algorithmically (controversial but measurable)

### Bluesky
- **Post**: 300 char limit. Earnest tone. No "thread starter:" announcements.
- **Threading**: reply to your own posts to chain
- **Custom feeds**: get included in popular niche custom feeds (the primary growth lever)
- **Hashtags**: 1-3 only. Topic tags, not spam.
- **No DMs** (as of 2025) — community is post-and-reply oriented

### Facebook
- **Reels**: cross-post from IG via Meta Business Suite (free)
- **Image post**: still works on FB unlike IG. Quote graphics, infographics, share-bait images.
- **Long-form text post**: works for older audiences — narrative storytelling, 200-500 words
- **Hashtags**: 1-3, low value but help group cross-discovery
- **Group posts**: own a niche FB Group → post there directly → drive page follows

### Pinterest
- **Pin title**: 100 chars max. Lead with keyword + benefit.
- **Pin description**: 100-500 chars. Weave 5-10 keywords naturally. Soft CTA.
- **Image**: 1000×1500 (2:3 ratio). Bold text overlay. Branded color palette.
- **Link**: lands on a destination — blog post, store, lead magnet. Pinterest is a click engine, not a vanity metric platform.
- **Boards**: 10-30 boards per page, each niche-specific. Pin to most-relevant board first, then 2-3 secondary boards (with 1-day spacing — rapid cross-pinning = spam signal).
- **Idea Pins**: native multi-page, evergreen. Lower click-through but high reach.
- **Rich Pins**: set up via domain claim — auto-pulls metadata from your site

## Repurposing flow (one master idea → 7 platforms)

### Master content unit: 30-second vertical video

**Adaptations**:

| Platform | Adaptation |
|---|---|
| TikTok | 30s 9:16 native. Trending audio overlay if niche-fit. |
| IG Reels | Same 30s 9:16. Custom cover. Caption restated for IG style. |
| YT Shorts | Same 30s 9:16. Title hook. #shorts tag. |
| FB Reels | Cross-post from IG via Business Suite. |
| Twitter/X | Embed 30s video natively (X plays inline). New caption. |
| Bluesky | Embed video. Text-first caption with question/observation. |
| Pinterest | Convert to 1000×1500 vertical static OR Idea Pin. Different headline. Link to blog/store. |

**Time cost**: 1 master edit (30 min) + 6 platform adaptations (5 min each) = ~60 min total. Without adaptation = 25-30 min, but reach drops 30-50% per platform.

### Adapting for long-form
- One YT 10-min video → cut into 3-5 Shorts/TT/Reels (best moments) + 1 Twitter thread (key insights) + 5 Pinterest pins (each insight as a pin)
- Long-form = pillar; short-form = distribution

## Account warming (first 30 days of new account)

New accounts get throttled. Don't post 5/day from day 1 — algorithm flags as bot.

### Week 1 — Setup phase
- Day 1: Profile setup only (no posts). Bio, profile pic, link.
- Day 2-3: 1 post total (introductory post). Engage with 20 niche accounts.
- Day 4-7: 1 post/day. Engage 30+ comments/day on niche posts. Follow 10-30 niche creators.

### Week 2 — Ramp phase
- 1-2 posts/day
- Comment on 50 niche posts/day
- DM 5-10 niche creators (genuine, not spammy)

### Week 3-4 — Production phase
- Hit target cadence (3-5/day for TT/IG growth)
- Reduce daily comments to 30-50
- Algorithm should now have categorized your account — you'll see niche-relevant FYP/Explore content

### Avoid in first 30 days
- Posting then deleting (massive trust hit)
- Buying followers/engagement (algorithm detects, suppresses)
- Following >50/day or >500 total in week 1
- Adding link in bio in first week (strong "this is a marketer" signal — wait until you have 100+ followers)

## Posting cadence reality check

| Phase | Cadence (TT/IG primary) |
|---|---|
| Days 1-30 | 1-2/day, focus on engagement |
| Days 31-90 (growth) | 3-5/day, push hard |
| Days 91-180 | 3-4/day, slight quality bias |
| 6mo+ (10k+ followers) | 2-3/day, more polish |
| 100k+ (mature) | 1-2/day, hero content |

Don't burn out at 5/day forever. Most successful theme pages reduce cadence as audience grows because each post earns more attention.

## Ban/strike avoidance

### Universal
- Never repost without watermark removal + re-edit (Tier 4 techniques in `scraping.md`)
- Original audio when possible — copyrighted music = strike or muted video
- Disclose AI content where required
- Don't engage in coordinated tagging/follow trains
- Avoid banned terms per platform (changes monthly — check creator forums)

### Platform-specific banned-term examples (research current list per niche)
- **YouTube**: in fitness — "weight loss" caps reach, "fat loss" passes; in finance — "guaranteed", "risk-free" trigger demonetization
- **TikTok**: ANY violence/sex/drug words even contextually = shadowban
- **Meta (IG/FB)**: in beauty — "results" + before/after = hidden; in finance — "rich", "wealthy" suppress
- **Pinterest**: weight-loss content heavily restricted post-2023

### What to do if banned/struck
- **Strike (warning)**: appeal once politely, accept once, fix the offending content type going forward
- **Account ban**: appeal once via official form. If denied: that account is dead. Don't try VPN workarounds — platforms detect and ban hardware.
- **Shadow ban (reach drop with no notification)**: stop posting for 48-72h, then post 1 high-quality non-controversial piece, monitor reach. If still throttled: posting break for 2 weeks usually resets.
- **The portfolio insurance**: own 2-3 pages per niche on multiple platforms so a ban on one doesn't kill the business

## Analytics — what to track weekly

### Per-post metrics (per platform)
- Reach / impressions
- Engagement rate (engagement / reach)
- Save rate (saves / reach) — strongest signal on IG/Pinterest
- Share rate (shares / reach) — strongest signal on FB/X
- Watch time / completion rate (video)
- CTR on bio link (if applicable)
- Comment quality (replies vs. emoji-only)

### Per-page metrics
- Follower growth rate
- Best-performing hook patterns (top 10 of last 30 days)
- Best-performing format (top 3 of last 30 days)
- Average reach per post (trending up?)
- Conversion to bio click / email / sale

### Cross-platform metrics
- Same content's performance across platforms — which platform is highest leverage for THIS niche?
- Reallocate effort to top-performing platforms quarterly

### Tools for tracking
- **Metricool** — built-in cross-platform dashboard
- **Native analytics** (TT Analytics, YT Studio, Meta Business Suite, X Analytics, Pinterest Analytics) — most accurate per platform
- **Spreadsheet** weekly: top posts, key metrics, learnings → feeds back into hook bank

## Outsourcing the publishing function

After 90 days running a page, outsource the production-publishing-engagement loop:

### Editor (production)
- $200-500/mo via Fiverr / OnlineJobs.ph / Upwork
- Provide: footage library, hook bank, brand template (overlay style, font, colors)
- They produce: 5-10 edited posts/week per platform
- You approve via shared Google Drive / Notion

### Community manager (engagement)
- $150-400/mo
- Reply to comments + DMs in your brand voice (provide voice guide)
- Engage on niche feeds 30 min/day
- Escalate sponsorship inquiries / business opportunities to you

### Total cost per fully outsourced page: $400-1000/mo
### Time freed: 5-7 hours/week

Math: page hits 10k followers, $2-10k/month revenue (affiliate + product + sponsorship) → $400-1000 outsource = 10-20% margin gone. Worth it if you're operating 3+ pages.
