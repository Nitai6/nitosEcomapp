# Account Management — Theme-Page Manual

How to set up, optimize, scale, and protect theme-page accounts as a portfolio.

## Profile optimization (per platform)

The profile is the **conversion point** — every viewer who hits your video → checks your bio → decides to follow OR click. Treat it as a landing page.

### TikTok profile
- **Username**: keyword + niche if possible (e.g. `air.fryer.recipes`, `desk.workouts`). Avoid numbers/underscores when possible.
- **Display name**: descriptive ("Air Fryer Recipes 🍳") — searchable
- **Bio**: 80 chars. Format: WHAT (niche) | WHO (audience) | CTA. Example: "Air fryer recipes for busy parents 👇 Free meal plan ↓"
- **Link**: 1 link allowed. Use Linktree / Beacons / your own /links page.
- **Profile pic**: bold, recognizable at thumbnail size. Logo or central niche image.
- **Pinned videos**: 3 pins. Use for: best-performing video, intro-to-page video, conversion video (explains lead magnet).

### Instagram profile
- **Username**: same as TT if possible (cross-platform consistency)
- **Display name**: keyword + emoji ("Easy Air Fryer Recipes 🍳") — IG search uses this
- **Bio**: 150 chars. Format: 1-line USP | 1-line audience callout | downward arrow → CTA
- **Link**: 5 links allowed via "Edit Profile → Links" (since 2023). Stack: lead magnet + product + newsletter + 2 affiliate
- **Profile pic**: same as TT
- **Story highlights**: 5-8 covers, branded. Categories: About, [Niche topic 1], [Niche topic 2], Reviews/UGC, Lead magnet
- **Pinned posts**: 3 grid pins (top of profile). Use for: best content + conversion content + new launch

### YouTube channel
- **Channel name**: niche-clear, brandable
- **Handle** (@yourname): same as IG/TT if possible
- **Banner**: 2560×1440. Show: who you are, when you post, value proposition. Mobile-safe area in middle 1546×423.
- **Channel trailer**: 30-60s for non-subs, plays auto on channel page. "What you'll get if you subscribe."
- **Featured video**: latest hero piece for returning subs
- **Channel sections**: organize playlists by topic (signals expertise to algorithm)
- **About page**: keyword-rich description (3-5 sentences with niche keywords for search)
- **Links**: up to 5 in profile + 1 banner link
- **Watermark**: subscribe icon overlaid bottom-right of every video (set in YT Studio)

### Twitter / X profile
- **Handle**: short, memorable, niche-aligned
- **Display name**: keyword in name ("@username — niche keyword")
- **Bio**: 160 chars. 2-3 lines. What + who + CTA + link.
- **Link**: 1 in bio
- **Pinned tweet**: best thread or lead-magnet thread
- **Header**: 1500×500. Lead-magnet visual or niche keywords visualized.

### Bluesky profile
- **Handle**: niche-aligned
- **Display name**: descriptive
- **Bio**: 256 chars
- **Pinned post**: equivalent to X
- **Custom domain handle**: own a custom domain → use as your handle (e.g. yourname.com) = signal of legitimacy

### Facebook page
- **Page name**: niche-keyword loaded
- **Username**: same handle across platforms
- **Cover photo**: 820×312. Lead magnet visual + CTA.
- **About section**: full description, contact info, hours if relevant
- **Templates**: Meta offers page templates (Business, Creator, Community) — choose Creator for theme pages
- **CTA button**: "Send Message" (drives DMs) or "Sign Up" (lead magnet) — set in page header

### Pinterest profile
- **Account type**: Business account (free, unlocks analytics + Rich Pins)
- **Display name**: niche keywords ("Air Fryer Recipes & Tips | YourBrand")
- **Bio**: 160 chars. Niche + value prop + soft CTA.
- **Profile pic**: logo-style
- **Boards**: organized by niche sub-topic, 10-30 boards
- **Featured boards**: 5 most relevant boards pinned to top of profile
- **Domain claimed**: claim your destination domain → unlocks Rich Pins + better analytics
- **Created tab**: showcases your original pins (different from saved/curated)

## The link-in-bio stack

The bio link is your highest-converting real estate. Don't waste it on a Linktree with 10 random links.

### Strategy: 1 primary CTA + 3-4 secondary

**Primary** (the lead magnet or main offer):
- Free guide / checklist / template (drives email signup)
- Newsletter ("Free [niche] tips weekly")
- Quiz (drives email + segments) — see `ecom-quiz` skill
- Direct product (only if niche is 100% commerce)

**Secondary** (in priority order):
- Latest content (blog post, YT video, longer resource)
- Most-asked-about product (affiliate or own)
- Community (Discord, FB Group, Slack)
- Other social platforms

### Tools for link-in-bio

| Tool | Cost | Strengths |
|---|---|---|
| **Linktree** | Free / $5-25/mo | Industry standard, lots of integrations, basic |
| **Beacons** | Free / $10-30/mo | Better creator features, Shopify integration |
| **Stan Store** | $29/mo | Built for digital product sales, auto-fulfillment |
| **Koji** | Free / $9/mo | Customizable widgets |
| **Custom (self-hosted /links page)** | $10-20/mo hosting | Full control, own data, fastest, best for SEO |

**Recommended**: Beacons or self-hosted. Linktree is the default but feels generic — and a custom domain on bio link converts 2-3× better.

### Tracking
- Use UTM parameters per link: `?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=bio&utm_campaign=mealplan`
- Track in Google Analytics / Plausible / Fathom
- Per-platform attribution = which page actually drives revenue

## Account portfolio strategy

### Why operate multiple pages

- **Risk diversification**: ban on one platform doesn't kill business
- **Niche A/B testing**: 2 pages in same niche, different angles, see which wins
- **Cross-promotion**: page A reposts page B's content, drives growth both ways
- **Sale-able assets**: theme pages sell for $1k-$50k+ depending on niche + size

### Multi-account ops on the same platform

**One person → multiple pages, same platform**:
- Each page must have its own phone number + email + IP for setup
- Use **separate browser profiles** (Chrome profiles, or app like SessionBox) per account
- Never log in/out rapidly between accounts on same device — algorithm flags
- For mobile: use **dual app feature** (Samsung), Parallel Space (Android), or separate physical devices

**Multi-account warning signs**:
- Posting identical content from 2 accounts on same platform = duplicate detection
- Following the same accounts from multiple of yours = network detection
- Comment spam between your own accounts = ban-trigger

**Safe portfolio approach**:
- Different niches per page (so content is genuinely different)
- Different posting times
- Different bio styles
- Cross-promote selectively (not every post)

### Outsourcing portfolio ops

Once you have 3+ pages running:

```
[YOU — 5 hrs/week]
  ├─ Strategy + niche research
  ├─ Hook bank curation
  ├─ Approve weekly content (sign-off step)
  └─ Sponsorship/business decisions
       ↓
[VA / EDITOR — 20-40 hrs/week, $400-800/mo each]
  ├─ Source content (per sourcing pipeline)
  ├─ Edit + format per platform
  ├─ Schedule via Metricool
  ├─ Reply to comments + DMs (provide voice guide)
  └─ Weekly analytics report
```

Hire from:
- **OnlineJobs.ph** (Filipino editors, $5-12/hr, English fluent)
- **Fiverr** (per-project basis, $5-50/video for editing)
- **Upwork** (broader, $10-50/hr)
- **Twitter / Reddit niche communities** (creator-savvy talent at premium rates)

Provide:
- Brand guide (1-page: voice, no-go list, visual style, font, colors, logo)
- SOP videos (30-min Loom walking through your editing process)
- Content templates (CapCut templates, Canva templates)
- Permissions doc (what they can post without approval, what needs sign-off)

## Analytics infrastructure

### What "good" looks like per platform (engagement-rate benchmarks for theme pages)

| Platform | Mediocre | Good | Great |
|---|---|---|---|
| TikTok | <3% | 5-10% | 15%+ |
| Instagram Reels | <2% | 4-8% | 10%+ |
| YouTube Shorts | <3% | 5-10% | 15%+ |
| YT long-form (CTR) | <4% | 6-10% | 12%+ |
| Twitter/X | <1% | 2-4% | 6%+ |
| Bluesky | <2% | 4-8% | 12%+ |
| Facebook | <2% | 3-5% | 8%+ |
| Pinterest (CTR on pin) | <0.5% | 1-2% | 3%+ |

### Setting up dashboard tracking

**Free version** — Google Sheet (template structure):
```
| Platform | Date | Post URL | Format | Hook | Reach | Eng% | Save% | Comments | Notes |
```
Update weekly. Sort by engagement. Patterns emerge after 30 posts.

**Paid version** — Metricool / Sprout Social / Buffer Analyze
- Cross-platform dashboard
- Auto-pulls metrics
- Best-time-to-post recommendations from your data

**Pro version** — custom Supabase dashboard pulling from each platform's API
- Once routines are built (per the broader paidads roadmap)
- Real-time dashboard with cross-page revenue attribution

### Reporting cadence

- **Daily**: glance at top post + reply to comments
- **Weekly**: 30-min review — top 3 / bottom 3 / hook patterns / next week plan
- **Monthly**: 60-min audit — niche health, follower growth trend, monetization KPIs, platform reallocation
- **Quarterly**: re-validate niche, consider new platform, adjust portfolio composition

## Monetization architecture

### Revenue stacks (combine, don't pick one)

```
TIER 1 — Audience monetization (most pages start here)
  ├─ Affiliate links (Amazon, niche-specific, ConvertKit, software)
  ├─ Sponsored posts (10k+ followers in commercial niche)
  ├─ Platform ad rev (YouTube YPP, FB In-Stream, X Premium share)
  └─ Tips/donations (Patreon, Buy Me a Coffee, Ko-fi) — niche-dependent

TIER 2 — Lead capture (highest LTV)
  ├─ Email list → newsletter ads → email-campaigns skill kicks in
  ├─ Quiz funnel → recommendations → store/affiliate
  └─ Community ($5-50/mo Discord/Skool)

TIER 3 — Own product
  ├─ Digital product (eBook, template, course, preset pack)
  ├─ Physical product (dropship or original)
  ├─ Service (consulting, done-for-you)
  └─ Software / tool

TIER 4 — Asset sale
  ├─ Sell the page itself (Flippa, Empire Flippers, Fameswap, MicroAcquire)
  └─ License the brand (rare but exists)
```

### Monetization gates per platform (don't activate too early)

| Platform | Native monetization | Affiliate-OK threshold | Sponsorship threshold |
|---|---|---|---|
| YouTube | YPP: 1k subs + 4k watch hrs OR 1k + 10M Shorts views | Day 1 | 10k+ subs |
| TikTok | Creator Rewards: 10k + 100k views/30d. TT Shop: 1k. | Day 1 (TT Shop) | 50k+ |
| Instagram | Reels Bonuses (intermittent), Subscriptions | Day 1 | 10k+ |
| Twitter/X | Premium share: 500 followers + 5M impressions/3mo | Day 1 | 10k+ |
| Bluesky | None native yet | Day 1 (newsletter only — no native commerce) | n/a |
| Facebook | In-Stream: 10k + 600k watch min/60d | Day 1 | 10k+ |
| Pinterest | None native | Day 1 (direct affiliate links allowed) | Rare |

### The "monetization-too-early" trap

- Adding affiliate links before 1k followers = "this is a marketer" signal → algorithm suppresses
- Wait until: page is categorized by algorithm (typically 20-30 posts) AND first viral post hits
- Then: ONE affiliate link in bio (not link soup), not in every caption

## Security + protection

### Account security checklist
- [ ] 2FA enabled on every platform (authenticator app, NOT SMS — SIM-swap risk)
- [ ] Backup codes saved in password manager
- [ ] Recovery email is one you control (not work email)
- [ ] Recovery phone is dedicated business line if possible (Google Voice works)
- [ ] Different password per account, generated by password manager
- [ ] Never use OAuth login through random apps
- [ ] Quarterly: review connected apps, revoke unused

### Backup + asset protection
- Download all original content monthly (Drive / Backblaze / iCloud)
- Export follower lists where possible (IG: not allowed; YT: subscriber count only; X: third-party tools risky)
- Maintain "what would I lose if banned today" doc → motivates email-list building
- The email list is the only audience you fully own — every platform can take followers; nobody can take your email list

### Email list as insurance
- Goal: convert 1-3% of followers to email subscribers per month
- Mechanism: lead magnet + bio-link + comment-trigger DM (see `social-funnel` skill)
- 100k followers + 2% capture rate = 2k subscribers in month 1, growing
- Once you have 10k+ on email: you have a business that survives any platform

## When to kill an account

Don't sunk-cost-fallacy a dying page. Kill criteria:
- 3 consecutive months of declining reach + engagement (not seasonal)
- Niche permanently saturated (10+ pages each at 500k+ in your exact angle)
- Banned/restricted with no path to recovery
- Off-brand for evolved business strategy

When killing:
- **Sell** if it has ≥10k followers in monetizable niche (Flippa / Fameswap / Empire Flippers / direct buyer outreach)
- **Pivot** the niche (rebrand) if followers are valuable but content angle is dead
- **Archive** (just stop posting) if neither sale nor pivot is worth the effort

## Page operator KPIs

Track per-page, monthly:

| KPI | Definition | Healthy |
|---|---|---|
| Follower growth rate | Net new follows / starting follows | 5-30%/mo (early), 1-5%/mo (mature) |
| Average reach per post | Total reach / posts published | Trending up or stable |
| Engagement rate | Total engagement / reach | See benchmarks above |
| Bio click rate | Bio clicks / reach | 0.5-3% (theme pages) |
| Email capture rate | New emails / bio clicks | 20-50% (good lead magnet) |
| Revenue per 1k followers | Monthly revenue / (followers/1000) | $5-100 (varies wildly by niche + monetization mix) |
| Time per post | Total operator hours / posts published | <30min (mature page with templates) |
| Cost per post | Tools + outsource / posts published | <$5 (mature) |

The key compound metric: **revenue per 1k followers**. Mature pages should hit $20-50/1k/month. A 100k-follower page = $2k-5k/month. Multiple pages × this = real income.
