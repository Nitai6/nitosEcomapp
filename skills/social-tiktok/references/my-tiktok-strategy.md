# TikTok Strategy — Owner's Brief

## North-star
"Fast-trending reels + product/niche edits. Always anchored to brand-strategy-blueprint."

## Cadence
1–2 posts/day = 7–14/week.

## Content split (rotate, not strict)
- ~50% niche-edit remixes (scraped trending → tweaked)
- ~30% product-shot (clean B-roll via screen-demo)
- ~20% founder POV (UGC from Telegram drop)

## Must-have rules
- Brand value prop in first 3s OR last 3s — non-negotiable
- 9:16 vertical, 15–60s
- Trending audio (rights-verified)
- Brand font + color on captions
- Forbidden words check (per brand-strategy-blueprint)
- IL prime hours 19:00–22:00 for scheduling

## Source signals (Apify scrape filters)
- Views > 50k in last 48h
- Engagement rate > 5%
- Niche hashtag list — owner-supplied per brand

## Edit guardrails
- Hard cuts on hook beats
- Subtitle every spoken word (Submagic auto)
- No watermarks from source
- Brand logo subtle bottom-right (last 2s only)

## Hashtag rule
3–5 niche + 2 trending. Mix matters — pure niche = no reach; pure trending = no relevance.
